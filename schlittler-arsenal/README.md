# Cam Schlittler's Three-Fastball Blueprint

**Published article:** [Cam Schlittler's Three-Fastball Blueprint](https://calledthird.com/analysis/schlittler-three-fastball-blueprint)

## The Question

Is Cam Schlittler's early 2026 dominance (22 K, 0 BB, 1.62 ERA through 3 starts) a hot streak or a genuine arsenal redesign?

## Key Findings

1. **Pitch mix transformation:** 4-seam dropped from 55.3% (2025) to 31.6% (2026); sinker surged from 7.1% to 30.3%; cutter rose from 18.9% to 29.4%. Three fastballs now at near-equal usage.
2. **The cutter remake:** +1.6 mph velocity, +7.2 inches of vertical break vs 2025 — from sweepy slider-adjacent to fastball-adjacent with legitimate ride.
3. **Usage balance is the rare trait:** 100th percentile among 2025 three-fastball RHP.
4. **Movement spread:** 66th percentile triangle area (above-average, not unprecedented). The combination of balance + spread is what's novel.
